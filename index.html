Venture Development Program

Idea Validation

BriXep Ecosystem identifies unregulated and independent entrepreneurs across India and transform them into established human capital through 6 months of intense training & education on viable concepts.

Startup Incubation

Post establishing human capital, The development department of BriXep provides 6 months of Incubation support to establish & foster the startup ventures.

Society Infrastructure

The Pre-Accelerator program put emphasis on pivoting the product based on pilot testing report received by the development department. Growth department undertakes to provide 12 months of pre acceleration services to ensure the best quality end product.

Technical Assistance

The sustainability department of BriXep offers 12 months of acceleration services to expand the venture globally and put major emphasis on vertical & horizontal integration/expansion.
